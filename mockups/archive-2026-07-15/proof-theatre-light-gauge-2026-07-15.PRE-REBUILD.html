04Boundaries

Honest limits

A useful instrument knows where it stops reading.

External evidence

Unresolved

Six fee-cap rules depend on evidence beyond the supplied statement. Those cases remain unresolved rather than being forced into pass or fail.

Structural boundary

Reported apart

Schema and protocol conformance establish valid structure. They do not establish merchant truth — the two verdicts are reported separately.

Input boundary

No inference

The verifier evaluates only the inputs it receives. It does not infer missing facts, completeness, or freshness.

The standard

Make the conclusion as inspectable as the claim.
