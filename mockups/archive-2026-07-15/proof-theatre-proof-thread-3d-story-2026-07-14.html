04 / Limits

Honesty belongs in the interface.

The unresolved edge is part of the product record, not a disclaimer hidden after the conclusion.

A useful verifier knows when to stop.
L-01

Six fee clauses need evidence outside the statement.

They are registered as not statement-checkable rather than forced into a false pass or fail.

L-02

The purchase-price base remains unresolved.

Base-derived fee conclusions are provisional until that denominator is supported by inspectable evidence.

L-03

Retrieval and classification remain advisory.

They can surface candidates. Deterministic rules and recorded evidence own the conclusion.

L-04

This page is a static simulated replay.

It uses dummy fixtures only. It does not access real merchant or platform data, call live services, or claim production impact.

Evidence over assertion

Make the conclusion as inspectable as the claim.

A compact proof can still preserve source, rule, scope, uncertainty, and replay—without asking a technical or non-technical reviewer to trust a black box.
