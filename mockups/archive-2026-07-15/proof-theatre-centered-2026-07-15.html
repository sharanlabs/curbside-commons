02 / How the check works

The evidence decides. AI helps explain.

The pass or hold result comes from the recorded values and a named, testable rule. AI may translate that result into clearer language, but it never decides whether the claim is true.

All values in this design study are simulated. It does not connect to a delivery marketplace, point-of-sale system, or live merchant account.
01 Read the two sources
The verifier receives a served claim and a merchant-controlled source record. It preserves both inputs so the final result can be traced back to what was actually compared.
02 Apply one registered rule
The rule LST-PRICE-CENTS-AS-DECIMAL detects a price serialized in cents where decimal dollars are expected. The rule identifies the mismatch; it does not silently rewrite the claim.
03 Attach the evidence
The result keeps the claim, merchant value, rule identifier, and severity together. A person can inspect that receipt before any operational action is considered.
04 Keep AI outside the verdict
Language models may later help draft a plain-language explanation. The pass/fail result remains deterministic, reproducible, and available without an AI call.
